Rusalka
Spirit Water Slavic
Rusalka is a water spirit from Slavic folklore, often associated with drowned maidens who haunt rivers, lakes, and forests. Though beautiful and enchanting, they are also dangerous, luring unsuspecting travelers to their doom. Rusalki embody both the allure and peril of nature.
